The Tallest Mountain in Africa.

                                           MOUNTAIN KILIMANJARO
1. Tanzania is the home to the highest Mountain in Africa-Mount Kilimanjaro is (5895metres),which is also the highest mountain in the world. Despite its highest Kilimanjaro is one of the world most accessible high summits, with most climbers reaching the peak(Uhuru peak),with little more stick, proper clothing and determination.
2. Climbing Mount Kilimanjaro is a virtual climatic world tour, from the tropical rainforest on the lower slopes and alpine heath to n almost bare alpine desert and finally the wintry arctic summit of snow and ice just 3 degree south of the equator.
3. The ascent of Kilimanjaro can be done through the following routes which are through; marangu route,machame route,mweka route,rongai route,Lemosho/shira and umbwe route.
a. MARANGU ROUTE
4. The Marangu or main route is by far the most popular way to the summit, the ascent is more gradual and no professional equipment is required.
5. The accommodation on the way up and down is provided in the mountain hut with all the basic necessities, there are beds at Mandara hut 2700m for 60 people and 120 beds at Horombo for 120 people 3720m which used also for descent, climbers are supplied with mattresses and pillows, but sleeping bags should be brought along, there are common dining halls, washrooms and toilets.
it usually takes 5 days/4night for the round trip, the trek can also taken 6days/5night to add acclimatization with an extra day at Horombo hut
                                  MACHAME ROUTE
This is probably the most scenic route up to Mount Kilimanjaro summit.
The Machame route is normally completed in a minimum of 6days/5 nights of the mountain.
                                 RONGAI ROUTE
There is only one route over on the north eastern side of the mountain, the Rongai route which is usually a 6 days and 5night climb, this route may not be quite as spectacular as the western route but it does usually have lower traffic than the other five night routes. The route offer different and amazing view on Kilimanjaro by approaching it from the north.
It is generally considered to be easiest of the quality route on the mountain ,Rongai route is the best route for the people who are looking for a decent quality experience and perhaps not absolutely confident about their fitness.
                           
                                         UMBWE ROUTE
This route based on the south, is a 6days/5night tour and is said to be the most scenic and difficult one offered on Kilimanjaro.
The first two days are extremely steep, muddy and generally strenuous making it only suitable for well trained mountaineers.
moreover the extra date can be added at Karanga valley.
                       
                                    LEMOSHO(SHIRA)ROUTE
This also knows for its longest distance to trek up Kilimanjaro, the Lemosho(Shira)route takes 7days/6night on the mountain.
The many changes of scenery and spectacular hike across the Shira plateau make this trek truly special.There are no huts on this route, the accommodation is in mountain tents.
NOTE
Despite of the all routes the best time to climb the mountain is between June and April.
