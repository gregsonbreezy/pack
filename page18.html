LakeManyara & National Park.

Lake Manyara national park is also amazing, its terrain is so diverse that its mammal and birds list are most impressive to observe, the park includes the northern and most of the western parts of the lake and its shores with a Westwood expansion of the top of the rift valley wall.
Here you will find The tree-climbing lion, numbers of elephant and resident in the park, buffalo are common and herd of 300-500 have been recorded by our safari guide, black rhinoceros are very common, leopards occur in most places and its usual to come cross them in the afternoon.
Manyara national parks is noted for its wealth of birdlife. At times the lake is visited by many thousands of Lesser Flamingos with a sprinkling of the larger species, others birds which can be seen is Moccoa and white-backed ducks are also resident ,pygmy Grocer, the chest-banded sand plover, also over 30 different birds of prey have been recorded during our safaris in the park including the palm-nut vulture, Ayres and crowned hawk eagles
